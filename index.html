About Us

Shreyash Classes started in 2008 and successfully completed 12 years of journey in Medankarwadi, Chakan. We are commited to ensure the coaching of syllabus provided by respective board(CBSE, Maharashtra Board, ICSE) within time frame as well as sufficient time is provided for revision of all topics. We believe teaching is two-way communication and understanding a concept is the most essential task.

Features

  • Online and Offline coaching facility with help of Interactive Panel Board and high resolution of projector
  • Apple chairs with adjustable wooden writing pad, comfortable for all age groups
  • Availability of E-books for CBSE and Maharashtra Board(SSC)
  • Provision of important notes
  • Limited students batch for personal attention towards each student
